## 1. Create a page

- Click &quot;Create&quot; and select &quot;Blank Page&quot; to create your first page.
- New pages are created as children of the page you are currently viewing.

## 2. Add to your page

- Click &quot;Edit&quot; to enter the Confluence editor and use the page layouts feature to structure your content using sections and columns.
- Use headings to format your text and drag and drop images into your page to provide visual interest.
- Click &quot;Insert&quot; and select &quot;Other Macros&quot; to add macros for navigation, special formatting and other media.
