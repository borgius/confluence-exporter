### **General**

The Fidelity Charitable DevOps Team is responsible for the overall continuous integration and continuous delivery process, Maintaining and Administrating Environments and infrastructure within Fidelity Charitable. We support customers of Jenkins, GitHub and Fidelity Charitable Environments . We fully support our continuous delivery pipeline for DIT, TEST, QA, and Prod.

If you're not a customer or need to engage us for the first time please email us at [NCSEnvSupp@fmr.com](mailto:NCSEnvSupp@fmr.com)

There is a Triage Meeting once each business day at 9:15am (ET). Tickets will be discussed and prioritized at this meeting. At this point tickets will be flagged for more information or assigned to someone with a due date and label.

### Jira Workflow

When a Jira ticket is opened it will follow the below workflow

**To Do → Triage → In Progress → Validation → Done**

**To Do**: where your request will land

**Triage**: At the triage phase our engineers will review the information, collect all the required/missing information from users

**In Progress**: Based on the priority and workload, the request will be assigned to one of the engineer and he/she will start working on the issue

**Validation: **The ticket is done and awaiting validation from the customer. Will be closed if no response

**Done**: Once the task is complete, the request will be marked Done(Resolved/Closed)
